About the Flask App

Welcome to our Flask App, where we predict student performance based on various features!

Our app utilizes machine learning algorithms to analyze data such as:
gender, race/ethnicity, parental level of education, lunch type, and test preparation course completion.
By considering these factors along with the students' scores in math, reading, and writing, our app can provide valuable insights and predictions.

Our goal is to help educators, parents, and students gain a better understanding of the factors that influence academic performance.
With this knowledge, we aim to support data-driven decision-making and interventions that can positively impact student outcomes.

Explore our app, discover interesting correlations, and gain insights into student performance. Get started today!
